About Nilambara Das

Nilambara Das, also known as Dr. Nagaraj Hegde, is a Bhakti Yoga practitioner and teacher from a traditional South Indian Brahmin family. His spiritual quest began in his teens with the teachings of Srila Prabhupada and ISKCON, leading him to Bhakti Yoga. In 2012, he was initiated into Gaudiya Vaishnavism by HH Radhanath Maharaj.

Nilambara Das holds a Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from the University of Alabama, where he founded the UA Vedic Society with his wife. His contributions to research, community service, and leadership earned him the title of Graduate Student of the Year in Alabama. Since 2018, they have been dedicated to building spiritual communities in the Netherlands, inspired by the teachings of the Bhagavad-gita and Srimad Bhagavatam.

In addition to leading ISKCON Amsterdam and serving on the Dutch National Council, Nilambara Das is involved in several start-up ventures. He has also invented an AI-assisted meditation device that tracks breathing and heart activity. For him, life is about embracing a wholesome spiritual experience and sharing that joy with others.
